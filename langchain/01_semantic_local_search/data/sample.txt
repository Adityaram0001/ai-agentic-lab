## Technical Specifications & Architecture

### The Quantum Core
The heart of the Zeta device is the Q-Core. Dr. Rostova’s patented design utilizes a synthetic diamond lattice infused with nitrogen-vacancy centers. These centers act as the qubits for energy storage. By applying a precisely tuned microwave frequency pulse (at 2.87 GHz), we can manipulate the electron spins into a "dark state," effectively trapping energy without the thermodynamic losses associated with traditional chemical bonds. 

The Q-Core operates at room temperature, a massive leap over the cryogenic requirements of early prototypes. The stabilization field is maintained by a proprietary AI-driven flux controller, which dynamically adjusts the magnetic field thousands of times per second to counteract environmental noise and prevent quantum decoherence.

### Thermal Management System (TMS)
While the quantum storage mechanism itself produces virtually no heat, the massive influx and outflow of energy during rapid charging and discharging create significant thermal stress on the conventional conductive pathways (the macroscopic interface). 
To solve this, Project Zeta employs a micro-vascular cooling network inspired by biological systems. A highly conductive, non-reactive dielectric fluid circulates through channels as thin as 40 micrometers, interwoven throughout the power delivery matrix. If the system detects a temperature spike exceeding 85°C, the fluid flow is instantly rerouted to the affected sector, dissipating the heat across the outer chassis. 

### Integration and Safety Protocols
Safety is paramount. The energy density of the Zeta Battery is roughly 400 times that of the best commercial lithium-ion cells. A catastrophic failure would result in an explosive release of energy equivalent to a small tactical munition. 
Therefore, Dr. Rostova mandated a three-tier failsafe system:
1. **Software Level:** The AI flux controller will intentionally break the quantum superposition if it detects structural damage to the chassis, safely bleeding the energy into a highly resistive internal carbon block as heat.
2. **Hardware Level:** Frangible connections in the macroscopic interface will physically sever the power draw if amperage exceeds safe limits.
3. **Chemical Level:** A rapid-expansion foam is housed in pressurized micro-canisters throughout the battery casing. In the event of a total breach, this foam expands instantly, hardening into an inert, non-conductive aerogel that smothers the Q-Core and absorbs concussive force.
